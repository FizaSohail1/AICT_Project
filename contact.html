Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have

What is the dress code?

We encourage smart casual to business attire. While jackets are appreciated for gentlemen, they are not required. We ask that guests refrain from wearing athletic wear, shorts, or flip-flops.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Our chefs are experienced in preparing dishes for various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-free options. Please inform us when making your reservation.

Is there a cancellation policy?

We kindly request 24 hours notice for cancellations. For parties of 6 or more, we require 48 hours notice. Late cancellations may result in a charge per person.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes, gift certificates are available for any amount and make perfect gifts for any occasion. Purchase them in person at the restaurant or contact us to arrange delivery.

Can I bring my own wine?

We welcome guests to bring wine with a corkage fee of $45 per bottle. However, if we carry the same wine in our cellar, we respectfully ask that you enjoy our selection instead.

Do you host private events?

Yes, we have elegant private dining spaces perfect for celebrations, corporate gatherings, and special events. Our events team will work with you to create a memorable experience tailored to your needs.
