Our Story

Our Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1985 by renowned chef Michel Dubois, La Maison has been a cornerstone of fine dining excellence for nearly four decades. What began as a small intimate restaurant has blossomed into one of the most celebrated culinary destinations in the city.

Our philosophy is simple yet profound: honor traditional French techniques while embracing innovative approaches. Every ingredient is carefully sourced from local farms and international purveyors who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

From our signature duck confit to our meticulously crafted seasonal tasting menus, each dish tells a story of dedication, artistry, and the relentless pursuit of perfection.

Meet The Team

Our Culinary Masters

Led by world-class talent, our kitchen team brings decades of Michelin-starred experience

Michel Dubois

Executive Chef & Founder

With over 35 years of culinary mastery, Chef Michel trained in Paris under legendary chefs and has earned three Michelin stars throughout his career. His innovative approach to French cuisine has redefined fine dining.

Sophie Laurent

Head Pastry Chef

Award-winning pastry chef Sophie brings artistry to every dessert. Trained at Le Cordon Bleu, her creations are both visually stunning and exquisitely delicious, completing each meal with perfection.

Antonio Rossi

Executive Sous Chef

Chef Antonio's dedication to seasonal ingredients and sustainable sourcing ensures every dish celebrates the finest flavors. His expertise in Italian and French fusion creates unique culinary experiences.
